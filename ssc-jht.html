SSC JHT (Junior Hindi Translator) is conducted by the Staff Selection Commission to recruit Junior Hindi Translator and Senior Hindi Translator posts.

SSC JE (Junior Engineer) is conducted by the Staff Selection Commission to recruit Junior Engineer (Civil, Mechanical, Electrical) in central government.
