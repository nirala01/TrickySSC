Speed Tricks

Shortcut Trick Library

500+ proven shortcut tricks to solve SSC CGL questions in under 30 seconds

⚡ Square of Number Ending in 5
📐 QUANT · MULTIPLICATION
n5² = n(n+1) | 25
Example: 75² = 7×8 | 25 = 5625
65² = 6×7 | 25 = 4225
This trick works for ANY number ending in 5!
⚡ Percentage ↔ Fraction
📐 QUANT · PERCENTAGE
1/6 = 16.67% | 1/7 = 14.28%
Memorize: 1/8=12.5%, 1/9=11.11%, 1/11=9.09%, 1/12=8.33%, 1/13=7.69%
Converting fractions is 3× faster than dividing!
⚡ Successive Discount Formula
📐 QUANT · PROFIT & LOSS
Net% = a + b - ab/100
Example: 20% + 30% discount
= 20+30 - (20×30)/100 = 50-6 = 44% net
Avoid lengthy calculations instantly!
⚡ Dice Opposite Face Rule
🧠 REASONING · DICE
Opposite = 7 − face value
Standard Dice: 1↔6, 2↔5, 3↔4
For non-standard dice, use unfolding method or elimination by adjacent faces.
⚡ Blood Relation Shorthand
🧠 REASONING · BLOOD RELATIONS
+M = Male · -F = Female
Draw a tree: Use + for male, − for female. Connect via = (married), ↓ (child). Solves 4-step chains in under 20 seconds!
⚡ Error Detection: S-V Agreement
📝 ENGLISH · ERROR DETECTION
Each/Every/Neither → Singular verb
Rule: "Each of the boys is (not are) present."
FANBOYS join two clauses — check both subjects separately. Covers 40% of grammar errors!
